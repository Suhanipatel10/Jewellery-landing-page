    Delivery Procedure

    You agree that the products are carefully inspected after our system has processed your order to ensure they are in good condition. Products are packaged and handed over to our reliable delivery partners after a final round of quality inspection.

    The package is then delivered as soon as possible to you by one of our delivery partners. Our delivery partners may contact you should they face any trouble locating your address or if there is an unforeseen delay.

    For all orders below Rs. 500, placed in India, a shipping fee of Rs. 75 is applicable.

    International Shipping

    To get the product, customers must pay the customs charges, import taxes, and other charges as may be required under applicable law, which will be collected on the Website and thereafter paid to the authorities.

    Packaging Details

    Our products are carefully wrapped in paper wraps before being placed in cartons for a safe delivery experience. We take great pride in the calibre of our packaging and strive not to use plastic.

    Tracking Information

    You will receive an email after your order has been shipped with information on the tracking number and the courier service handling your order. You can check the status of your package 24 hours after your order is dispatched from our warehouse.

    Delivery Timelines

    Most orders are typically shipped out in 1-2 business days (excluding Sundays and public holidays) and delivered as per the estimated time of delivery provided on the product page/product cart.

    However, product delivery may get delayed due to reasons beyond our control or attributable to logistics, national and public holidays, riots, political disturbance, natural calamity, act of God, or any other such force majeure event.

    You agree that no refund or cancellation will be allowed due to any delay in delivering the products within the estimated time of delivery provided on the Website and/or communicated to you.

    Shipments

    You acknowledge that one order with more than one product may be delivered in multiple shipments due to different warehouse locations. All our orders are verified using codes sent via text messages and/or emails at the time of delivery (“Delivery Acceptance Codes”) to ensure secure delivery of orders.

    In the event that you do not receive a Delivery Acceptance Code at the time of delivery, you agree to provide the delivery personnel with a document that acts as an identity proof of the person who placed the order to complete the order.
